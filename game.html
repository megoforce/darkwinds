Introducing

Darkwinds is an Online Collectible Card Game, that uses the Ethereum blockchain for card distribution and storage.

Like other card games, Darkwinds is half competitive game, and half collectible game. While some players might want to buy and trade cards to complete their collection, others will build a competitive playing deck in order to become the best Darkwinds player!

Darkwinds draws inspiration from the myths and realities that pirates faced on their journeys and taking it to the realms of fantasy, where numerous deities, human and non-human pirates, giant creatures and powerful artifacts and spells converge.

In this unruly landscape you must take on the role of Captain, gathering a crew that can face rival Captains that prowl around the Seven Seas, looking to become the most feared pirate in the world.

Sailing inspires the main force in the game: Winds. There are four essential winds, North, South, East and West.

North Wind

Embodies the spirit of life and creation, the vital force that moves all creatures and pirates to fight for survival and the perpetuation of their own kin.

South Wind

Incarnates the spirit of death and destruction, the essence that drives the inhabitants of this world to long for death and unravel chaos.

East Wind

Epitomizes the spirit of nature and wilderness, contained in the creatures and living beings that feel part of the living world.

West Wind

Manifests the spirit of magic and the unearthly, enclosed in those who defy the limits of nature and take course into the depths of the unknown.

Building a Deck

To play the game, you need to build a deck with exactly 10 cards, which you can get buying booster packs from the store or by buying single cards from third parties such as OpenSea.

Anatomy of a Card

A card has the following elements: name, cost, wind, artwork, text, and if it is a buccaneer or creature it has damage and defense.

The Game

The game is played between two Captains that control a 10-card deck each. Captains take turns to draw a card from their deck, play cards and then attack their rival or their rival's cards.

Card Types and Abilities

Taunt

When a buccaneer or creature has the taunt ability, you cannot attack your rival directly. This means that in order to attack a rival that has a card with taunt on his board, you must kill this card first.

Charge

When a buccaneer or creature has the charge ability, that card can attack the same turn it was played.

Assault

When a card has the assault ability, it can deal damage to any enemy card or the rival captain, bypassing any active taunt ability.

Heal

When a card has the heal ability, it can heal a friendly card or yourself.

Summon

When a buccaneer or creature has the summon ability, it summons the card stated on the ability directly into play like it was played from your hand.

Requires

When a buccaneer or creature has the requires ability, you cannot play it unless you comply with the abilities requirement.
