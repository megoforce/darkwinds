Darkwinds is a multiplayer trading card game with Ethereum cryptocollectibles.

Available for Firefox and Chrome with the Metamask extension.

Cryptocollectible cards


Each Darkwinds card is a ERC721 Ethereum cryptocollectible, which means you are free to transfer, trade, sell outside the game.

Each card is a unique token stored on a Smart Contract, and only 1,000,000 first edition cards will be generated.

Active development

While you can purchase the final cards and play online, we are playtesting the game rules in open beta until the consolidation date (October 2018). We'll be also adding new features each week in progress towards our milestones.

Pricing

Access to Dark Winds is free. You'll only spend money while purchasing cards using your ETH wallet provided by Metamask. Create the ultimate strategy and you can go to the top of our Ranking.
