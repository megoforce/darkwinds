A blockchain trading card game

Buy, trade and sell cards in the Ethereum network and become the great grand master of the forbidden seas.


Play Now

Available for Firefox and Chrome with the Metamask extension.

Cryptocollectible
cards


Each Darkwinds card is a ERC721 Ethereum cryptocollectible, which means you are free to transfer, trade, sell outside the game.

They are generated by a Smart Contract, and only 1,000,000 first edition cards can exist.

Active Development

While you can purchase your Darkwinds cards now, online servers are in an "open beta" stage where some rules are being balanced or redefined.

The match

Arr!!! Setup your deck and challenge other pirates in a deadly duel! Every victory will add up to your reputation until you become the Grand Master of the Seven Seas! Yo-ho-ho, and a bottle o' rum!

Pricing

Access to Dark Winds is free. You'll only spend money while purchasing cards using your ETH wallet provided by Metamask.
